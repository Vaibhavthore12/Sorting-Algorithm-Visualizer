SortCraft Quick User Guide

SortCraft PRO lets you step through, analyze, and hear sorting algorithms in action.

  • Play / Pause: Start or freeze the visualization step loop anytime.
  • Step Forward: Execute exactly one comparison/swap step at a time for deep analysis.
  • Array Patterns: Test algorithms against Random, Worst-Case Reversed, Nearly Sorted, or Mountain Peak distributions.
  • Audio Synth: Real-time pitch tones reflect bar heights. Mute or adjust volume from the top header.
  • Themes & Light Mode: Toggle between Dark/Light modes or switch color palettes from the header dropdown.
